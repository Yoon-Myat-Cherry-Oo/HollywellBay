What We Do?

Nowadays, golf courses are becoming popular venues for business meetings and enhancing partnership. A golf tournament will be an excellent idea to get to know your prospects or clients in a totally social environment. This is where you can build relationships through golf and expect better business relationships.

Golflux is here to assist you in successfully organizing this important event. We are pleased to give you valuable advises on planning as well as gladly take all the responsibilities of preparing, organizing and administrating. These following describe what our Golf Tournament Organizing Services can do for you in each procedure. Take a look at it and feel free to contact us for further discussion.

Preparing

  • Assistance in invitation process.
  • On-line registration available.
  • Supply all gifts and prizes.
  • Prepare and supply of necessary items (name badges, draw sheets, etc.) for the day.
  • Decoration.
  • Other facilities.

Administrating

  • Media Pickup and drop off.
  • Supply staff in administration and security.
  • Control player registration.
  • Organize professionals to play with guests throughout the round.
  • Provide guests with on course refreshments. Collect and check scorecards.
  • Compilation of prize winners list.
